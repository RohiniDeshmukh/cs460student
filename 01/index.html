RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

Rome is an interactive web movie called "3 Dreams of Black," which offers a visually stunning experience set to the song "BLACK. Three distinct dream sequences are used in this innovative cinematic endeavor to present a story while smoothly merging 2D and 3D visuals. Utilizing WebGL's capabilities in a web browser environment, the movie uses real-time rendering of visual models. Viewers can actively interact with the information during this immersive experience by changing the perspective, rearranging items, and even making brand-new objects. "Rome" offers a dynamic and engaging cinematic journey by skillfully fusing a range of interactive demos featured on the website.

TECHNOLOGY: JAVASCRIPT, HTML5, 2D GRAPHICS, 3D GRAPHICS, three.js, GLTF2Loader.js
